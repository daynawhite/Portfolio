About Me

As a former middle-school math teacher, I relish the idea of a new challenge. With my goal-oriented personality and an adventurous spirit, I constantly seek to learn new things and accomplish new goals.

The hats I have worn also include educational technologist, landscape designer, salesperson, remote learning specialist, stay-at-home mom, and coach.

My love of nature comes from growing up in Colorado; but I nurture my love of live music in the Austin, Texas area, which has been my home since college. I have raised three amazing children, all of whom also appreciate live music and proudly call themselves math nerds!

In my free time, I enjoy all things outdoors. Some of my favorite activities include running, rock-climbing, camping, hiking, snowboarding, martial arts, gardening, and landscape design.

As often as possible, my dogs accompany me on my adventures in the outdoors, but my chickens prefer to stay at home!

During my less active moments, I enjoy reading, learning to play a musical instrument or speak a new language, experimenting in the kitchen, or crafting something from recycled materials.
